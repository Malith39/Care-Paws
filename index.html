About Us

At Care paws, we are dedicated to giving every animal a second chance at finding a loving home.
Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome pets in need, providing them with the care and compassion they deserve. With a team of passionate volunteers and foster families, we work tirelessly to ensure that every pet receives the love, attention, and medical care they require. Together, we strive to make a positive impact on the lives of animals and the families who adopt them.

Join us in our mission to save lives and create forever homes for pets in need.

Find a Best Vet for Your Pet

Welcome to the veterinary section of Care Paws! Here, we prioritize the health and well-being of our rescued animals. Our dedicated team of experienced veterinarians and compassionate staff work tirelessly to provide top-notch medical care to every furry friend that comes through our doors. From routine check-ups to emergency surgeries, we ensure that each animal receives personalized attention and treatment tailored to their specific needs. With our commitment to excellence in veterinary care, you can rest assured that your beloved pets are in good hands.
